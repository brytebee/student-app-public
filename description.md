# Native Tutor AI: Detailed Project Description

## 🌟 Vision & Mission

**Native Tutor AI** is dedicated to the preservation and revival of Nigerian languages through the power of conversational AI. Our mission is to halt the linguistic erosion of heritage languages by providing an infinite, proactive, and culturally nuanced coaching partner. We believe that a language is the soul of a people, and our vision is to ensure that no Nigerian language is "heard for the last time."

## 📍 Current Status & Milestones

- **Production-Ready MVP**: The core conversational loop is functional, supporting foundational interactions in major Nigerian languages (e.g., Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo).
- **Private-Public Architecture**: Successfully implemented a dual-app structure that balances user privacy (local SQLite "Private Brain") with community-verified linguistic accuracy (GitHub-hosted "Public Heart").
- **UI Refinement**: The student application has been updated with a premium "Academic Indigo" design language using Jetpack Compose.
- **Engine Logic**: The core language processing logic is hosted in a private repository (`language-engine-private`), ensuring proprietary orchestration remains secure while serving the public-facing application.

## 🏗️ Technical Challenges

Building an AI tutor for low-resource languages on standard mobile hardware presents unique hurdles:

1.  **Model Scarcity**: AI models optimized for Nigerian languages are extremely rare.
2.  **Mobile Constraints**: Finding or training models that can run efficiently on NPU/CPU of mid-range mobile devices is a significant technical bottleneck.
3.  **Acoustic Architecture**: Developing a smooth multi-acoustic receiver and sender mobile architecture that handles real-time audio without lag is complex.
4.  **Voice Configuration**: Supporting a diverse array of voices within the app requires extensive configuration and fine-tuning to ensure naturalness and regional accuracy.

## 🚀 Strategic Direction & Partnerships

To overcome these challenges, Native Tutor AI seeks to collaborate with global leaders in AI and cultural preservation:

- **Meta (No Language Left Behind)**: Seeking alignment with the Meta "1,000 Languages" project to leverage high-quality datasets and models for under-represented languages.
- **Google**: Aiming for support from Google’s language preservation initiatives, given our shared goal of organizing the world’s information (and heritage) to make it universally accessible.
- **Hardware Manufacturers**: Partnering to optimize our "Eco Mode™" for specific chipsets (MTK, Snapdragon) to ensure thermal safety in hot climates.

## 🎮 Community & Gamification

We are implementing a **Gamification System** that empowers both communities and individuals to take ownership of their linguistic heritage. This system allows:

- **Collective Training**: Communities can contribute and verify linguistic data to feed and train the models.
- **Engagement Loop**: Users are rewarded for consistent practice and for contributing "Gold Standard" corrections to the Consensus Portal.
- **Preservation through Participation**: By making language learning a social and rewarding experience, we turn the tide from language disappearance to active growth.

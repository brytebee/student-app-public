## 🌍 The Impact
With over **500 languages** at risk of disappearing, Native Tutor AI provides:
*   **Scale**: Automated, 24/7 access to high-quality tutoring where human teachers are scarce.
*   **Cost**: $0 deployment using free-tier orchestration and local NPU/CPU processing.
*   **Sovereignty**: Ensures that linguistic data remains in the hands of the community via our private-public hybrid architecture.

## ✨ The Vision
Many Nigerian languages are categorized as "threatened" by UNESCO. Native Tutor AI changes the narrative from "hard to learn" to **"impossible to forget"**. Our tutor doesn't wait for you; it reaches out, starts the conversation, and coaches you through the rhythm and tones of your ancestors.

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🎙️ Proactive AI Persona
Unlike standard language apps, Native Tutor's AI initiates interactions based on real-world context.
*   **Contextual Invitations**: "Barka da asuba" (Good morning) prompts when you wake up.
*   **Gentle Interruptions**: Real-time correction of tonal nuances (Yoruba) or vowel harmony (Igbo).

### 🔋 Eco Mode™ Thermal Safety
Optimized for the African mobile landscape. The app monitors NPU/CPU thermal state, automatically throttling to a low-energy state when devices hit 42°C, ensuring safety and longevity for mid-range hardware.

### 🛡️ Private Brain, Public Heart
Our unique dual-app architecture ensures:
1.  **Student Privacy**: All personal learning gaps and context are stored in a local-only SQLite database.
2.  **Community Consensus**: Verified linguistic data is pulled from our public [Consensus Portal](https://github.com/brytebee/community-consensus).

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
*   **UI/UX**: Jetpack Compose (Material 3) with a custom "Academic Indigo" design language.
*   **Orchestration**: Google Antigravity Agentic Framework.
*   **Safety**: Kotlin-based Thermal Surveillance logic.
*   **Storage**: On-device Encrypted SQLite.
